The Teach Nations Story

A journey of faith, transformation, and impact across nations

Our Journey

Teach Nations began with a vision to raise a generation of biblically grounded, purpose-driven leaders who would transform their communities. From humble beginnings, God has expanded our reach across schools, churches, and communities in multiple nations.

2020

Vision & Launch

Teach Nations was founded with a mandate to disciple nations through biblically grounded training and community transformation.

2021

Bible School on Wheels

Launched the mobile Bible School platform, bringing discipleship training directly to local communities and church hubs.

2022

Schools Partnership

Partnered with Scripture Union to deliver Schools Discipleship Programs, reaching hundreds of students and school leaders.

2023

Community Programs

Expanded into community development with health care visits, educational support, and livelihood projects for vulnerable families.

2025

TEN Fund Launch

Students launched the TEN Fund to sponsor 100 children per term, demonstrating our commitment to generosity and sustainable impact.
