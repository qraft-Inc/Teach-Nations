Schools Discipleship Programs

We shape the next generation through faith-based education and mentorship. Teach Nations runs Schools Discipleship Programs across multiple countries in partnership with organisations like Scripture Union, empowering teachers, chaplains, and high school students to live out their faith with conviction and compassion.

Our initiatives

Training and mentoring school patrons and deans

We equip school leaders to facilitate effective Bible study groups and spiritual mentorship programs that foster authentic faith communities in schools.

Biblical materials and leadership courses

Tailored resources designed for students that blend scriptural teaching with practical leadership development, helping young people understand their identity and calling.

Career guidance and mentorship

Structured sessions where students receive career counseling and mentorship from experienced professionals and pastors who help them discover and steward their God-given purpose.

Impact

Through these programs, we are nurturing a generation of young people who are spiritually strong, academically focused, and socially responsible—equipped to transform their schools and communities.
