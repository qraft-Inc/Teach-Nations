Course overview

Foundational teaching on following Jesus and discipling others. This course covers core discipleship practices, mentoring models, and practical application for local communities.

Syllabus

  1. Week 1: What is discipleship?
  2. Week 2: Following Jesus in daily life
  3. Week 3: Mentoring and small groups
  4. Week 4: Multiplication and leadership

Delivery

Available via online communities, local hubs and church trainings.
