About Teach Nations

Teach Nations is a Christ-centered initiative dedicated to discipleship, career development, and leadership training for emerging and established leaders across the world. Through Teach Every Nation (TEN), we deliver biblical, transformational, and practical courses that equip leaders, students, and pastors to live out their faith with excellence and purpose.

Our mission is to raise a generation of believers who are spiritually grounded, professionally competent, and socially responsible leaders who transform their communities through knowledge, integrity, and service.

Our Focus Areas

1. Bible School on Wheels (BSOW)

The Bible School on Wheels is our foundational discipleship and leadership training platform. It offers a structured pathway for leaders, pastors, and students to study and grow in their faith through biblically grounded and practical courses delivered through online communities, local training centers, and church-based hubs.

Sample BSOW courses:

  • Survey of the Bible
  • Forgiveness: 70x7
  • What on Earth is God Doing in My Life?
  • Double Your Productivity

2. Schools Discipleship Programs

We shape the next generation through faith-based education and mentorship. Teach Nations runs Schools Discipleship Programs across multiple countries in partnership with organisations like Scripture Union, empowering teachers, chaplains, and high school students to live out their faith with conviction and compassion.

  • Training and mentoring school patrons and deans to lead Bible study groups.
  • Providing biblical materials and leadership courses tailored for students.
  • Offering career guidance and mentorship sessions to help students discover their calling.

3. Community Development Programs (Good Works)

We believe faith is demonstrated through action. Our Community Development Programs extend Christ’s love beyond classrooms into everyday life through educational support, health care visits, support for vulnerable mothers, and livelihood projects.

  • Educational Support: providing school supplies and learning materials.
  • Health Care & Compassion Visits: weekly hospital visits for mothers and children.
  • Support for Mothers: care and material assistance for vulnerable mothers in Kyebando and nearby communities.
  • Community Empowerment Projects: outreach and livelihood initiatives that promote dignity and hope.
TEN Fund (2025)

In 2025 we launched the TEN Fund — a student-led initiative to sponsor 100 children in school each term with a contribution of UGX 100,000 per child. This program is fully driven by Teach Nations students and reflects our heart for generosity and sustainable community transformation.

Our Commitment

Teach Nations exists to build a global movement of biblically grounded, purpose-driven, and socially responsible leaders. Through our Bible schools, discipleship programs, and community outreaches, we demonstrate the love of Christ in word and deed—transforming individuals, schools, and communities across nations.
