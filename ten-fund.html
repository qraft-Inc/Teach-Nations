TEN Fund — Sponsor a Child

The TEN Fund is a student-led initiative to sponsor 100 children in school each term with a contribution of UGX 100,000 per child. Your gift helps provide school fees, supplies and hope.

How to contribute

You can donate UGX 100,000 to sponsor a child through MTN Mobile Money:

Option 1: Quick Dial

Click the button below to dial directly and get the merchant code:

Dial *291# to Donate

Option 2: Manual Steps
  1. Dial *291# on your phone
  2. Select option 3 (Pay Merchant)
  3. Enter merchant code: 288475 (Teach Nations)
  4. Enter amount: 100000
  5. Confirm the payment

Student leadership

This program is led by Teach Nations students and managed through our finance office. Every contribution goes directly to sponsoring children in need of educational support.
